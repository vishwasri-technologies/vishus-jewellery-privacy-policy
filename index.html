Vishu Jewellery App Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number for authentication purposes
  • Non-Personal Information: Device information and browsing activity to enhance your user experience
  • Demographic Information: Such as postcode, preferences, and interests
  • Other information relevant to customer surveys and/or offers.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to improve user experience and analyze usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We require this information primarily to provide better service by understanding your needs along with the following reasons:

  • Internal record keeping
  • Improvement of our products and services, including optimizing jewellery browsing
  • Customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Sending notifications about new collections, special offers, and updates. Users can opt out of such communications at any time
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with third parties in the following cases:

  • Internal record keeping
  • Improvement of our products and services, including optimizing jewellery browsing
  • Customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Sending notifications about new collections, special offers, and updates. Users can opt out of such communications at any time
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

4. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or misuse, we implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure authentication processes. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. User Control Over Personal Information

You may choose to restrict the sharing or use of your personal information in the following ways:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information.
  • Disable location tracking.
  • Manage notification preferences directly within the app settings.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Any modifications will be communicated to users and will take effect immediately upon posting.
